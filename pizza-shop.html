Pizza Shop

Welcome to the Pizza Shop! Enjoy a variety of delicious pizzas made with fresh ingredients. Whether you prefer classic Margherita, pepperoni, or a veggie delight, we have something for everyone. Don't forget to try our special dipping sauces!

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Pizza Shop is located near the main entrance, adjacent to the Coffee Shop. It's the perfect spot to grab a quick bite before continuing your zoo adventure.

Opening Hours: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Come and enjoy a slice of pizza while taking in the sights and sounds of the zoo. Our friendly staff is here to serve you with a smile, ensuring you have a delightful dining experience.
