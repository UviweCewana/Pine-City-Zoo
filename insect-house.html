Insect House

Discover the fascinating world of insects at the Insect House. Here, you can observe a variety of insects in their natural habitats, learn about their life cycles, and understand their roles in the ecosystem. From butterflies to beetles, the Insect House offers an educational experience for all ages.

Today's highlights:

  • Butterfly Garden - Watch butterflies flutter around in a beautiful garden setting.
  • Ant Colony - Observe the intricate workings of an ant colony.
  • Beetle Display - Learn about different species of beetles and their unique characteristics.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Insect House is located near the main entrance, adjacent to the Butterfly Garden.

Visit us to explore the wonders of the insect world and gain a deeper appreciation for these often-overlooked creatures.

Whether you're a nature enthusiast or just curious, the Insect House is a must-visit destination at Pine City Zoo. Don't miss out on this unique opportunity to connect with nature and learn about the incredible diversity of insects.
