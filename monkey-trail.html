Monkey Trail

The Monkey Trail is a scenic path that winds through the lush greenery of the zoo, offering visitors a chance to observe monkeys in their natural habitat. The trail is designed to provide an immersive experience, allowing you to walk among the trees and enjoy the sights and sounds of the jungle.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail is located near the main entrance of the zoo, adjacent to the Insect House. Look for the signs that guide you to this unique trail.

Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Come and explore the Monkey Trail, where you can witness the playful antics of our resident monkeys. The trail is suitable for all ages and provides a perfect opportunity for photography and wildlife observation. Don't forget to bring your camera to capture the moments!
