Coffee-shop

Relax and enjoy a cup of coffee while watching the world go by. Our coffee shop offers a variety of beverages and snacks to keep you energized during your visit.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The coffee shop is located near the main entrance, adjacent to the amphitheater.

Opening Hours: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Come and take a break in our cozy coffee shop, where you can relax and recharge before continuing your adventure at the zoo. Whether you prefer a hot coffee, a refreshing iced drink, or a light snack, we have something for everyone.

Don't forget to try our special coffee blends and homemade pastries, made fresh daily to ensure the best quality and taste. Our friendly staff is here to serve you with a smile!
